During February and March I polished my demo, and this demo will be listed in Itch.io search and GameJolt (since I decided to release also in GameJolt).

Also, both in TGPY Game and SCP: Site Online has the possible copyright problem, related to CC-BY-SA license (the first has SCP-173 from SCP Unity, and the latter is an SCP game) (I discussed that problem in 2024.11.08 blog)
